Hostage Rescue/Bomb
By Michael Visser aka 3D_Mike
(3DMike@nmtrix.com)

Somewhere in a small country research on a
nuclear weapon has almost been completed. In
a desperate attempt to stop completion of the
research a small terrorist fraction has
captured the factory chief executives and
attempt to cut off the only road to the plant.

Counter-Terrorists:
-Prevent the Terrorists from bombing.
-Rescue the hostages and guide them safety.

Terrorists:
-Bomb tunnel exit.
-Prevent the CTs from rescuing the hostages.
